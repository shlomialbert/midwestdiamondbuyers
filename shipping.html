Shipping & Security

Shipping Information

All orders are handled with care and fully insured.

At Midwest Diamond Buyers, we operate from our business location at 29 E Madison, Chicago. Every shipment is fully insured to ensure your valuable items are protected.

All shipments are handled by experts and insured agencies for secure and reliable delivery, rather than standard mail. We prioritize safety, speed, and transparency for every transaction.

You can expect professional handling, tracking updates, and peace of mind knowing that your jewelry, diamonds, or watches are transported securely from our facility to your destination.

Insurance & Liability

  • We are a registered business and accountable for every order.

After your order is shipped, you will receive tracking details so you can monitor your shipment at every stage.
