WHY

Midwest Diamond Buyers

Location Evaluation Shipping

Midwest Diamond Buyers was created to offer an alternative to the traditional retail experience, one built around discretion, clarity, and real market knowledge.

We are not a jewelry store and we are not brokers. We are specialized buyers who focus on natural diamonds, fine jewelry, luxury watches, and precious assets with established resale demand.

Every evaluation is conducted privately and professionally, with offers based on current global pricing rather than inflated retail assumptions.

Our process is straightforward: we review submissions the same day, meet by appointment in a private office setting, explain value transparently, and settle immediately if you choose to sell.

No pressure. No public counters. No uncertainty.

Our Heritage

Based in the heart of Chicago's Loop, Midwest Diamond Buyers was built on a simple belief, trust is earned through transparency. We are not a traditional retail store, we are specialized buyers with deep expertise in diamonds and vintage jewelry. Each piece valued not just for its weight, but for its history and craftsmanship.

Ethical Excellence

We focus exclusively on natural diamonds. No lab-grown, no synthetics, only stones formed by the earth over millions of years, valued for their rarity, integrity and enduring worth.
